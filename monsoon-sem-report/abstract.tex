\begin{abstract}

As Android continues to evolve as a Universal Operating System, the risk related to security also continues to grow. We study, in detail, the Android OS security model and how android permissions and other security options integrate together to make Android a secure OS. Next, we study various rooting methods; review and compare some of them. We also analyze, various traits of the rooting methods, that can be used to detect and identify rooted devices.


\end{abstract}
